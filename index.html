Our Fairytale

Summer 2020

After the world experienced a crazy first half of 2020, Tori and Jake found hope in each other online where they started talking everyday.

July 2020

To begin a series of many fun dates together, the couple shared their first official date at Belleria in Hubbard, Ohio.

8/30/20

After coming to Pittsburgh to visit Tori for the first time, Jake officially asked her to be his girlfriend. This marked the beginning of their long distance relationship.

2021 - 2023

From Hubbard to Findlay to now Pittsburgh, they have been living together as their love and relationship blossom, with Tori working as a 4th grade teacher and Jake as a professor.

8/30/23

3 years after their first date, Tori came home from work anticipating a normal anniversary celebration, but instead found flowers and Jake holding an envelope with a poem inside. After reading the poem, Tori saw Jake on one knee with a ring, where asked her to marry him. With no surprise, she said yes!
